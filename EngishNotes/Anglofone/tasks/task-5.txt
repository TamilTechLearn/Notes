    II Fill in the blanks 
        1.  They  watched the movie, so they don’t want to watch it again.(Have watched, Watched) 
        2.  They have missed the bus because they arrived late at the station.(missed,have missed) 
        3.  She has lost her keys, and now she can’t open the door.(lost, has lost) 
        4.  He sent me an email but didn’t get a reply.(Sent,have sent) 
        5.  He has tried a new restaurant and said the food was amazing.(has tried,tried) 
        6.  She baked a delicious cake for her friend's birthday last week.(Baked,has baked) 
        7.  They have read this book for years, but they still love it.(have read,read)
